Data Scraping and Research Specialist

Data Extraction
Posted

Specialized profiles can help you better highlight your expertise when submitting proposals to jobs like these. Create a specialized profile.

Worldwide
TechNexus Venture Collaborative, a Venture firm in Chicago, needs help with a data scrapping and research project to support one of their companies in the RV industry.  This project will require looking up locations of campgrounds and RV dealerships across the United States, logging the information in an Excel sheet, and reporting back the findings.

Expectation: Complete research and data scrapping request below while effectively communicating project timelines and asking questions in a timely manner

Request: Data scraping for locations of campgrounds and RV dealerships in the US

Output: Excel file including required fields of Name, Type, and Location


Project Instructions:
- Scrap below websites for all campground and RV dealerships in the United States:
- Please create two excel sheets for (1) Campgrounds and (2) Dealerships
- Campgrounds
        
         - Use https://koa.com/campgrounds/ website to scrap all KOA locations and indicate in the KOA column
      
- Dealerships
         -  Scrap for RV Dealership, Camping World locations, and Cruise America locations using the below websites
         - RV Dealers: https://www.rvt.com/dl_list.php
         - Camping World: https://rv.campingworld.com/state-directory
         - Cruise America Locations: https://www.cruiseamerica.com/rv-rental-locations

Fields Required for Logging:
-  Name: Business name of the campground or dealer
- Type: Campground, Dealership, Rental Dealership
- Flag: Create a flag/indicator if the location is either a KOA campground, Camping
  World, or Cruise America
- Location: Address (including zip code)  or GPS coordinates
        - If the GPS coordinates are provided, Address does not need to be completed
